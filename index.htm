Welcome to Tank Tag!

Tank Tag is a fast-paced, action-packed game where two tanks battle it out in a world filled with obstacles and challenges. Use your strategic skills and quick reflexes to outmaneuver your opponent and emerge victorious.

**Instructions:**
- **Show/Hide Debug Info:** Press 'I' to toggle visibility.
- **Tank 1 (Grey):** Move with WASD, shoot with V.
- **Tank 2 (Red):** Move with arrow keys, shoot with 'Enter' key.
- **Objective:** Destroy the enemy tank 5 times to win.
- **Health Restoration:** Return to your base for rapid healing or to the enemy base for slower healing.
